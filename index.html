About ShopERP

Transforming businesses through technology

ShopERP was founded in 2020 with the mission to provide small and medium businesses with enterprise-grade tools at an affordable price. We believe every business deserves access to powerful technology that helps them grow.

Our team of developers, designers, and business experts have created a solution that combines the power of large-scale ERP systems with the simplicity and usability that smaller businesses need.

  • Easy to implement and use
  • Scalable for growing businesses
  • Affordable pricing plans
